Welcome to YPS!
Yavatmal Public School is managed and run by Shri. Jawaharlal Darda Education Society Yavatmal. It was a vision of the famous freedom fighter- Shri. J.L Darda to enlighten this tribal area and provide all facilities to the students of Yavatmal. It was the initiative of the founder President- Shri. Vijay J. Darda- M.P(Rajya Sabha) to materialize the dream of “Babuji”. This is the first private unaided CBSE co-educational English medium school in the district of Yavatmal. It was established in April 2006. The school started in the present premises with 279 students from classes I to V. Now the school has grown up to a full fledged tree with classes from pre-primary to XII, which is fully recognized by CBSE. The present strength is about 1400 students.

Our Inspiration(Babuji)
We are deeply inspired and motivated by the late freedom fighter our, Dear and loving Babuji Shri. Jawaharlal Darda. A man with a very distinct & clear vision for the people of the society. He knew if anything that could change India – it was Education alone. Education had to be made available and spread in the far flung suburbs and poor villages, this great endevour had to been given a shape and the seeds had to be sown. It was the initiative of the Founder President of Shri. Jawaharlal Darda Education Society’s, Shri. Vijay J. Darda (M.P. Rajya Sabha) & Chairman cum Managing Director of the Lokmat Group of News Papers, who in the year April 2006 started the First CBSE School in Yavatmal.




Facilities Available:
The school always strives for Quality Education and keeps upgrading with innovative ideas and brings in the facilities of Global standards.
  • This is the first school having fully digitalized class rooms for classes I to XII.
  • Xseed-Idiscoveri pattern of teaching is implemented for Pre-primary to class IV.
  • Along with it we have also the facility of E-learning (NIIT), E-blocks Education techniques.
  • The school has separate fully equipped laboratories for Physics, Chemistry and Biology.
  • A computer lab with more than 30 systems and broad band facility.
  • All the class rooms are equipped with LCD projector- Laptops and UPS as well as a DG set to cope with the power crisis.
  • Apart from all these the school gives room for special trainings in music, dance and art to reinstate the Indian culture. It also imparts special training in Chess, Football, Cricket and other physical activity.
  • The teachers here are given training from time to time from CBSE-Delhi, FJEI-Pune,Xseed-Idiscoveri and other agencies as well.
  • An Auditorium with capacity of 400 students.
  • It has a huge library with more than 7589 books and references apart from 5000 specimen.
  • Bansal Satellite Learning Program for Engineering/ Medical started in April 2013.
  • New Staff Room, Dance Room and Music Room.
The school has been certified with A Grade from an NGO-FJEI - Pune in terms of providing quality education and facilities.
